Better World Book Summaries


The Almanack Of Naval Ravikant

Eric Jorgenson

Constitutes experiences and thoughts of Naval Ravikant on mainly wealth and happiness from several sources such as his Twitter, essays, podcasts etc.



Flowers for Algernon

Daniel Keyes

Charlie Gordon, a mentally disabled man, becomes a genius with an operation, a genius who doesn't know who he is.



The Razor's Edge

William Somerset Maugham

WWI Pilot Larry, traumatized by the events he encountered during his service, chases spirituality.
